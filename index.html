1. Salaar Part 1 Cease Fire - 113.2 millions & BOX OFFICE COLLECTION : ₹ 700 crores 😱

   

& SalaarPart 1-Ceasefire:            The story is set in a fictional, dystopian city-state called Khansaar, ruled by monarchy and torn by political power struggles. “Varadha” (the prince of Khansaar) faces a deadly threat when powerful ministers and relatives plot a coup. To survive and reclaim his right, he calls upon his childhood friend “Deva.” The film explores themes of friendship, loyalty, betrayal, power — and is drenched in large-scale action, violence, political intrigue and intense drama

  • Prabhas — plays “Deva” (Devaratha “Deva”)

  • Others: Jagapathi Babu, Bobby Simha, Sriya Reddy plus a supporting ensemble cast.

2. KGF Chapter 2 - 106.5 millions& BOX OFFICE COLLECTION : ₹1200 crores 😱

   

& KGF Chapter 2:         The series follows the life of “Rocky,” a man born in poverty, who rises through crime and struggle to become a powerful figure controlling the gold-mine empire tied to Kolar Gold Fields. It combines raw action, gritty underworld drama, high stakes, power struggles — a “rags-to-riches + revenge/ambition” storyline. This gritty, stylized storytelling and larger-than-life hero character helped KGF stand out — especially among regional-language films. The lead is Yash, who plays the central character (known popularly as “Rocky”).Other important cast members: Srinidhi Shetty, and various supporting actors across both parts.Crew: Prashanth Neel (writer / director), cinematography by Bhuvan Gowda, music by Ravi Basrur

  • Vasishta N. Simha – Kamal
